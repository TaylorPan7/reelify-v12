Welcome to the Reelify support center. Find answers to common questions, access tutorials, or get in touch with our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the Video Generator?

Simply navigate to the Video Generator page, enter a topic or description in the text field, choose your preferred settings, and click "Generate." Our AI will create a video based on your prompt.

What file formats are supported for uploads?

We support MP4, MOV, and AVI for video uploads, and PNG, JPG, and JPEG for image uploads. Maximum file size is 500MB for videos and 20MB for images.

How accurate are the auto-generated captions?

Our AI-powered captioning typically achieves 95%+ accuracy for clear audio in English. You can always edit the captions manually if needed.

Can I use the generated content commercially?

Yes, depending on your subscription plan. Our Pro and Enterprise plans include commercial usage rights for all generated content. Please refer to our Terms of Service for detailed information.

How are credits calculated for different operations?

Video generation uses between 1-5 credits depending on length and quality settings. Image generation uses 1 credit per image. Captioning uses 1 credit per minute of video.

How do I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?

Go to your account settings and select "Subscription." From there, you can manage your plan. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
